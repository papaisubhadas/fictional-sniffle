Our Services

Wholesale & Reseller – Terminations

Sales

We have a team of trained specialists who are dedicated to reach out to potential customers promoting products – finding them the most suitable options through cross selling or upselling

We have different teams reaching out to cover both the B2B and B2C customer base

Ours teams main objective is to find the best product match for the customers.

Lead Gen

We take pride in our team dedicated to identifying potential customers and sale prospects for marketing purposes. Appointment setting: Our associates contact the customers scheduling appointments for representatives can meet them at a time suitable to their convenience.

Survey

The foundation of growth for any company is to study insights from the target customer base and analyze the same to understand the market. And the same is facilitated primarily by means of data collection.
Our team of experts is trained to design objective specific questionnaires, reach out to maximum number of participants and gather their responses.
The data analyses of the summarized data collected from the survey plays a key role in the client’s decision making about implementing upgrades or changes to the products or services customizing them to the customers.

Call Centre-Terminations

In this fast paced life, everyone’s objective is to make life easier for our clients To achieve this feat, the main objective is to satisfy the customer by being present to handle their queries. We at RGT are dedicated to the following:

Customer support – Responding to customer queries, providing the best possible assistance

Query Resolution - Providing the most suitable resolutions for their concerns

Coordination – Forwarding queries to the necessary departments, ensuring timely resolution

Sales Enquiry – Upselling or cross selling to potential customers

DID & CLI

Data plays a crucial role in strategic decision making for any organization. Researching, collecting and managing data is an extremely time consuming task.

Hence our role is to take charge of the same for our clients, providing them for the results which would lead to maximizing product efficiency.
